It has been more than eight months since my last post. Far from doing nothing, I just couldn't find anything to write about. This post is an attempt to break this silence by sharing some of what happened during that period.

After yet another round of interviews, I decided to comeback to the East Coast for the summer. This follows two internships in the Bay Area after which I thought it would be interesting to try something different. So I accepted an offer at Bloomberg, in the middle of New York City. This would give me a unique opportunity to experience the city so nice, they named it twice. And I loved it.
